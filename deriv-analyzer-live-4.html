The 5 Rules
1
Trade with rules. Set 3–4 solid personal rules. Rules give structure and edge.
2
Leave emotions at the door. Let your carefully crafted rules decide. Trust the system not your feelings.
3
Master risk management. Know exactly how much you're willing to lose. When you lose, accept it and handle it like a pro.
4
Don't overtrade. Set daily target and stop loss. Hit either — stop trading. Come back when relaxed.
5
Never revenge trade. When a trade goes south — take a breather, review what happened, come back stronger.
Golden Principle
"No setup is mandatory.
Preserving capital is part of the strategy."
Common Mistakes
1
Missing entries due to market change — wait for the next market.
2
Unrealistic profit targets — greed. Set achievable targets. Increase stake gradually.
3
Not setting stop loss — have a number of runs you're willing to take.
4
Too many indicators — have precise strategies only.
5
Trading without rules — never trade on hope or gut feeling.
6
Not keeping a journal — track every trade. You will learn more from your losses than your wins.
